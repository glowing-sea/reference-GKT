Why my CPU can outperform my GPU for this model?

The model is extreme small

1. Your GKT configuration is tiny:
* hidden_dim = 32
* embedding_dim = 32
* concept_num = few hundred
* MLPs with 2-3 layers of 32-64 units
* GRU of 32 units per concept

GPU gains only appear when:
* large batches
* large matrices
* heavy convolutions
* wide layers

2. GPU cost per kernel launch is high

3. GKT heavily uses Python loops and non-vectorised operations:

* _aggregate()
* _agg_neighbors()
* update()
* predict()

Inside these are:
* many indexing operations, like torch.where and index_put

4. The DataLoader & padding overhead is CPU-bound

Padding or masking performs on CPU before moving to GPU
